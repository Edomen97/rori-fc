Our Vision & Mission

👁️ Vision

To showcase Hawassa’s vibrant health, wellness, and sports ecosystem to Ethiopia, Africa, and the global arena, while raising an exemplary, fit, and active generation.

ከተማችን ሀዋሳን በስፖርትና በጤና ዘርፍ ለኢትዮጵያ፣ ለአፍሪካ ብሎም ለዓለም ማሳየት እና ለሀገር የሚጠቅም ጤናማ ትውልድ ማፍራት።

🚀 Mission

Building disciplined, physically robust citizens who uphold high moral ethics, encouraging professional experience-sharing platforms among cross-city hotels, and empowering individual athletes to advance academically, financially, and occupationally.

አባላት በትሬይኒንግ ሰዓት በመገኘት፣ እርስ በራስ በመከባበር እና ኢትዮጵያዊ ስብእናቸውን ጠብቀው ብቁ እንዲሆኑ ማድረግ፤ እንዲሁም የሆቴሎች እርስ በርስ ልምድ ልውውጥን ማጠናከር።

Club Objectives

01

Physical Fitness & Health

Promoting regular physical exercise, well-being, and a highly active lifestyle for all club members.

02

Professional Growth

Encouraging athletes to continuously transform their lives through structural focus on education, personal financial literacy, and secure job placements.

03

Hotel Network Collaboration

Establishing collaborative platforms for localized hotels to interact, form robust networks, and engage in modern workflow experience exchanges.

04

Generational Impact

Molding a physically sound, capable, and highly ethical generation ready to serve and bring immense value to our country.

Club Constitution & Rules

✓
Absolute Punctuality (በሰዓቱ መገኘት)

All members must strictly report on time for designated training blocks. Tardiness directly compromises team efficiency.

✓
Mutual Respect (እርስ በራስ መከባበር)

Fostering mutual honor, deep professional respect, and harmony remains mandatory among all participating athletes, staff, and leadership.

✓
Upholding Ethiopian Character (ኢትዮጵያዊ ስብዕና)

Every member serves as an ambassador, bound to reflect foundational Ethiopian cultural ethics, dignity, and sportsmanship both on and off the playing field.
